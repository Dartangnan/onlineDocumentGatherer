Future Functionalities

Internal Revenue Service

Issued by the Federal Revenue Service, this negative certificate will be released when the taxpayer 's tax compliance with the federal tax credits administered by the Federal Revenue Service of Brazil - RFB

Employer Regularity Consultation

Issued by Caixa Econômica Federal, this certificate is a condition for the entrepreneur to be able to relate with the Public Administration bodies and with official credit institutions, as indicated by the issuing page itself.

State Debt Clearance Certificate

This certificate will be issued when the taxpayer's tax compliance with the state tax credits administered by the State Finance Secretariat is verified. Therefore, each state has its own criteria to allow the issuance of the document.

Negative Certificate of Labor Debts

Issued by the Superior Labor Court, this certificate is based on information submitted by all 24 Regional Labor Courts in the country and will be negative if the individual or legal entity is not registered as a debtor with the National Bank of Labor Debtors.

Negative Certificate of Securities and Real Estate Taxes

This certificate is a document issued by the Municipality of each municipality. It is based on data contained in the Register of Taxpayers' Securities, which includes Individuals and Legal Entities that carry out activities in a given municipality.
